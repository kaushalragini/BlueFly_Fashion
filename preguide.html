PRE-OWNED CONDITION GUIDE

At Bluefly, we only work with top, industry-trusted designer & pre-owned vendor partners to ensure that you are getting authentic, high-quality designer products.

Follow the guide below on our rating system for vintage & pre-owned items. We do not sell anything that we deem unusable, deteriorated/damaged beyond repair or anything that would fall below satisfactory condition.

RATING CONDITION
NEVER CARRIED Never been used. Item is immaculate.
EXCELLENT Very minimal signs of wear and in almost-new condition
VERY GOOD Gently used. Slight signs of wear but in overall good condition
GOOD Item may have signs of wear but overall product is in good condition
